About Us

Our NGO is dedicated to preserving and promoting cultural heritage in Lebanon. Our mission is to raise awareness of the rich cultural heritage of Lebanon and to encourage its preservation and protection.

Our Values

We believe in the importance of cultural heritage for future generations and the need to protect it from degradation and loss. Our values include:

  • Preserving cultural heritage for future generations
  • Encouraging the promotion of cultural heritage
  • Raising awareness of the value of cultural heritage
  • Supporting the preservation and protection of cultural heritage sites and monuments

Our History

Our NGO was founded in 2019 with the goal of promoting cultural heritage in Lebanon. Since then, we have worked tirelessly to raise awareness of the value of cultural heritage and to support its preservation and protection. Our efforts have included organizing educational events, supporting restoration projects, and working with local communities to promote cultural heritage.

Our Impact

Since our founding, our NGO has made a significant impact on the promotion and preservation of cultural heritage in Lebanon. Some of our accomplishments include:

  • Organizing educational events for schools and the public
  • Supporting the restoration of several cultural heritage sites and monuments
  • Working with local communities to promote cultural heritage
  • Raising awareness of the importance of cultural heritage through media and public events
